SIGClinical is the ACL Special Interest Group on Clinically Related Language, which is dedicated to fostering engagement among members of the ACL, clinicians, and clinical researchers in studying approaches to processing clinically related language.

It is the sponsor of the Workshop on [Computational Linguistics and Clinical Psychology (CLPsych)](https://clpsych.org/) and the [Clinical Natural Language Processing Workshop (ClinicalNLP)](https://clinical-nlp.github.io/).
